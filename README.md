# Product with Bharath 🚀

A personal brand and learning platform focused on Product Management, AI, Product Thinking, and Case Studies.

## 🌟 About

Product with Bharath is a platform where I document my journey of becoming a Product Manager while helping students and beginners learn Product Management through practical examples, real-world case studies, frameworks, and resources.

My mission is to make Product Management easier to understand and accessible to everyone.
